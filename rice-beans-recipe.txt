Ingredients:
 - 1 cup of white rice
 - 1 cup of red beans
 - 2 oz of tomato sauce
 - 2 tsp of sofrito
 - 1/2 slice of ham

Directions:
 1. In a medium pot, bring 9oz of water to a boil.
 2. Add the rice, stir, and bring back to a boil. Stir for a minute, cover the pan and leave at low heat for 15 minutes.
 3. In a small saucepan at medium heat, add sofrito, tomato sauce, and diced ham. Heat for 2 minutes.
 4. Add beans to saucepan along with 8oz of water. Stir, and leave at same heat for 10 minutes.
